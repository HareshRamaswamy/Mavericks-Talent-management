Mavericks Talent Management Application

MTMA is a web application with a fresh approach towards Mavericks Talent management with new ways of Monitoring, Status tracking and Management through visualisation of the large chuncks of data generated during the Mavericks Training Program.

It utilizes the Machine Learning, Data Visualisation, and Classification techniques to provide an in depth, real-time information about the batches in the Mavericks Training Program.

From contrasting maverick performance to pre-defined benchmarks to classifying them to a role which is most suited for them based on their performance during the training period and the Company's requirement.

It does all this while providing the data in a simple, clearer and easy to understand Visualizations to the Management team, Trainers, and even the Mavericks so that they can keep track of their progress and be the best version of themselves.

Future Scope:

  • Email Prompts: Using this web application, reports can be generated and sent to admin by the trainer.

  • Classification of mavericks for a particular job: Using ML algorithm, this can be implemented and it will be helpful for the admin to allocate stream for all the Mavericks based on their forte.

  • Report generation based on custom options: Using this functionlity, instead of batch-wise or course-wise reports, the user can generate their own report by choosing the horizontal and vertical axis parameters themselves.
